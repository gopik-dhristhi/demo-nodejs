About the Milky Way Galaxy

Basic Details:

Type: Barred spiral galaxy

Diameter: ~100,000 light-years

Number of stars: 100–400 billion

Distance to Earth: We are inside it! Our solar system is about 27,000 light-years from the galactic center.

Age: ~13.6 billion years

The Milky Way is our home galaxy, containing the Solar System along with billions of other stars and planets. It appears as a faint band of light in the night sky due to the concentration of stars in the galactic plane.
